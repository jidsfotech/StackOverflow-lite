How do I simulate placeholder functionality on input date field?

It's impossible to use placeholder on date fields but I really need it. I want two date inputs with texts "From" and "To" on each one as placeholders.

Question by Jidsmooths Jan 18, 2018 at 11:23 PM

2 Answers

When you simulate placeholder behavior in incompatible browsers, be mindful of how that will affect your validation. Set ad efault value that disappears onfocus and reappears onblur.
